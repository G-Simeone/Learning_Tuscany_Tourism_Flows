Personas

To identify the similarity between tourist trajectories, we cluster individuals by their spatial trajectories or sequences categorised by the locations they visit during a trip. W use the optimal matching algorithm (OMA) to compute distances between sequences (cost of insertion, deletion and substitution to match two sequences). The distance matrix between sequences are clustered using k-medoids algorithm.

Key characteristics of the methodology:

  • Timing (e.g: When does a tourist visit Florence)
  • Duration (e.g: How long does a tourist stay in Florence)
  • Order (e.g: Where did they come from and where do they go next)
