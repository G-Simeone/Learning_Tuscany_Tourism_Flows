In the last summer season (Jun - Aug 2017)> roughly 31 thousand tourists visited Tuscany from France. The data shows us 3 clusters. Each line in the graph above represents a cluster's typical path that tourists from France followed. These paths are displayed as differently-coloured lines in the map here above. 
The first cluster, which we named Florence and Rome, spends on average 3.1 days in Italy, 2.7 of which in Tuscany. When in Tuscany, Florence and Rome spend at least a half day in Florence. The second cluster, which we named Florence and Siena, spends on average 11.6 days in Italy, 11.3 of which in Tuscany. When in Tuscany, Florence and Siena spend at least a half day in Florence, Siena, The Coast, Pisa, and Lucca.
