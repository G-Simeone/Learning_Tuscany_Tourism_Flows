In the heatmap above, we can see the density of the visits of all tourists belonging to the <i>Central Italy Trips</i> cluster. The darker the colour, the more Central Italy Trips visited that municipality during the pre-summer season (May 2017). As we can see from the  heatmap, the majority of the tourists from  this cluster visited Firenze (95.0% of the tourists in this cluster). The next most visited municipalities are Cortona (37.0%), and Montepulciano (36.0%).
