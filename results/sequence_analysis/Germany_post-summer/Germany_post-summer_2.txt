In the heatmap above, we can see the density of the visits of all tourists belonging to the <i>Florence and Rome</i> cluster. The darker the colour, the more Florence and Rome visited that municipality during the post-summer season (Sep - Nov, 2017). The next most visited municipalities are b'Firenze' (3.0%), and b'Barberino di Mugello' (3.0%).
