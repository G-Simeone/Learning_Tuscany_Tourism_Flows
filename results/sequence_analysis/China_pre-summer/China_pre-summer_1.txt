In the heatmap above, we can see the density of the visits of all tourists belonging to the <i>Around Tuscany</i> cluster. The darker the colour, the more Around Tuscany visited that municipality during the pre-summer season (May 2017). As we can see from the  heatmap, the majority of the tourists from  this cluster visited b'Firenze' (72.0% of the tourists in this cluster). The next most visited municipalities are b'Bagno a Ripoli' (39.0%), and b"Rignano sull'Arno" (39.0%).
