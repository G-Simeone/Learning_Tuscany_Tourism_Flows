In the heatmap above, we can see the density of the visits of all tourists belonging to the <i>Tuscany Explorers</i> cluster. The darker the colour, the more Tuscany Explorers visited that municipality during the post-summer season (Sep - Nov, 2017). As we can see from the  heatmap, the majority of the tourists from  this cluster visited b'Firenze' (15.0% of the tourists in this cluster). The next most visited municipalities are b'Siena' (9.0%), and b'Impruneta' (8.0%).
