In the heatmap above, we can see the density of the visits of all tourists belonging to the <i>Florence and Cinque Terre</i> cluster. The darker the colour, the more Florence and Cinque Terre visited that municipality during the post-summer season (Sep - Nov, 2017). As we can see from the  heatmap, the majority of the tourists from  this cluster visited b'Firenze' (22.0% of the tourists in this cluster). The next most visited municipalities are b'Impruneta' (9.0%), and b"Rignano sull'Arno" (8.0%).
